College admission for all degrees encompasses the evaluation of applicants based on academic performance, standardized test scores, and extracurricular activities. Understanding the nuances of college admissions can empower applicants to effectively navigate the system and enhance their chances of acceptance.

Web Design / Graphics Design / UI / UX Design
Web design, graphic design, and UI/UX design focus on creating visually appealing and user-centric digital experiences. Web design encompasses layout and aesthetics for websites, while graphic design involves creating visual content for various media.
